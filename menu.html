Featured Dish

Thali

This meal is a vibrant spread of Indian dishes that promise a rich culinary experience:

  1. Naan Bread: A basket of soft, chewy flatbread, perfect for scooping up sauces or curries.
  2. Pakoras: Crispy, deep-fried fritters garnished with fresh herbs, served alongside a colorful salad mix of shredded carrots, cabbage, and lettuce.
  3. Curries: Several bowls of thick, aromatic curries, each topped with julienned ginger and fresh cilantro for a burst of freshness.
  4. Green Chutney: A small bowl of tangy, mint-cilantro chutney, ideal for dipping and complementing the spices.

The variety of textures—from the crunch of pakoras to the softness of naan—and the blend of aromatic flavors make this a delightful dining experience!
